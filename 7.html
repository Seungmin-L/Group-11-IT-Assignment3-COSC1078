SCOPE & LIMITS

 


      The project’s food delivery app aims to deliver a friendly user experience. This means designing a user-friendly interface, contrasting but not overwhelming colours and concise and useful tabs. Namely home, restaurants, location, settings and user account. Designing and implementing the aforementioned features would be ideal however, often not practical due to limited resources and time frames. Compulsory features which must be delivered at the end of this project should at least be the ‘restaurant’, ‘location’ and ‘account’ management tab. At least a proof-of-concept can be created. 


     The project’s food delivery robot aims to deliver a fast, reliable and safe experience, for every stakeholder making contact with it. Deliverables which we inspire to accomplish are the safety and reliability aspects of the robot. Safety is our number one priority for the robot. We want the robot to be very aware of its surroundings such as objects, especially people. The team seeks for a balance between rapid yet safe service, so speed limitations will be most likely be delivered during this implementation. Secondly, the team will emphasize the importance of the reliability of the robot. Not only concerning regular wear and tear, but also the reliability of its GPS tracking. The team will endeavour to produce a robot which is accurate when navigating to and from destinations. Safety and reliability must at least be delivered in this project, other technical features successfully implemented, such as near-face communication for food security are ideal.
