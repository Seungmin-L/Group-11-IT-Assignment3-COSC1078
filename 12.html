GROUP PROCESSES & COMMUNICATION

 


 

Q. HOW WILL YOUR GROUP COMMUNICATE?


        From the outset all team members agreed to communicate using the MS Teams application. There are chat function tabs within the application, which we use to contact one another regrading work, organising meetings and recording meetings. The ‘Teams’ tab is another form of communication used to communicate assignment work to one another as well as other relevant documents regarding the project.


 

Q. HOW OFTEN WILL MEETINGS TAKE PLACE?


   From the beginning of the project, team members discussed appropriate dates and times to schedule meetings. All members were happy to conduct 4x 25-minute meetings. The meetings spanned across four different days and mostly took place in the afternoon or evenings.




Q. WILL MEETINGS BE FACED-TO-FACED AND

     WHAT TECHNOLOGIES WILL BE USED?


   Due to the current health crisis, meetings were to take place online, using the MS Teams application.




Q. WHAT WILL YOU DO IF YOU HAVE A GROUP MEMBER 

     WHO DOES NOT RESPOND TO COMMUNICATIONS?


   Initially, it was agreed for all team members to be punctual to the agreed dates and times regarding meetings and short reply times. This verbal agreement was implemented to reduce the risk of slow replies or late or no shows to agreed meeting schedules. However, over the course of the project there have been several occasions regarding late replies and meeting push backs due to members not being present. And when team members did not respond to communications, other present team members would agree to push meetings/tasks back, and wait for all members to be present, before further decision making.
