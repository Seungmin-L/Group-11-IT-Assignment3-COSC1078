OVERVIEW

 


 

TOPIC


     In this group project we will continue to build upon our initial project idea, the food delivery robot and food delivery mobile phone app. This time, we seek to implement at least two competitive advantages in terms of quality to help us stand out from the competition. 


     During this project we will be discovering our manufacturing partners concerning app development and robot manufacture. We seek to find reliable and competitive partners, so that we can maintain a productive and low-cost relationship. The project will mainly focus on the usability of the food application as well as the reliability and accuracy of the delivery robots. The consumers use the food application to order food, and the restaurants utilise the robots to deliver foods quickly and effectively. The overarching goal of this entire project is to produce a functioning and competitive food delivery service which can turnover profit.


 

MOTIVATION


The motivating factor for this project is that there is a high use and demand for food delivery app services because the current global pandemic forced people to stay at home. As a result, some people must use food delivery apps to get their hands on their favourite food or food that they are craving. This project is interesting because it seeks to implement unique features that helps to differ itself from the competition. It has an alternative delivery option where the user can choose to have a robot make the delivery instead of a human which has its benefits. This project is in line with the current IT trend where people are ordering food more often as the number of food delivery app users has increased by 21.7% in comparison to last year. 


     A successful project; usable food app and reliable robot, would entertain employers when the project is shown off. Features which make it stand out such as NFC and Fast Wireless charging, will impress employers because these aspects have yet to be incorporated into competitor business models. Employers enjoy intelligence and innovation because it helps businesses grow and a chance for innovation, through people who have built projects through their ideas.




LANDSCAPE


Similar services that are currently available include UberEATS, Deliveroo, Menulog and DoorDash. These businesses have also incorporated a food app and robot to deliver the foods over short distances. 


     Our project contains two competitive advantages over the competition. The first difference point of difference between our food delivery app and others is that our app has a phone tap function that is identical to NFC, it allows the customer to open the robot by simply tapping their phone on the top of the robot while the app is open to be granted access to open and take their food. The benefit is easy to use, fast and improves food security inside the robot. This prevents food contamination and robot tampering. 


     The second point of difference is that our food delivery robot offers fast wireless charging. Every robot purchased by or rented to restaurants will come with a wireless charging pad. The idea of this is to have the robot automatically return to the wireless charging pad after each food delivery to maintain high battery percentage. The benefit of this is convenience through automation. Delays when staff forget to charge the robot is no more and staff time is better utilised elsewhere instead of monitoring battery life. These are the two points of difference that have been introduced since the last assessment task.
