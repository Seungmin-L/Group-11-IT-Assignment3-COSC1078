RISKS

 


      A potential risk that the project may encounter is that the food delivery robot may run into some technical issues while making a delivery, where the navigation system on the robot is not going to the correct customer address. This can lead to losing consumer trust, business opportunity and the likelihood of them being a return customer because they were not left with a good impression. 


     The second risk of this project could potentially be choosing the wrong app development partner. By choosing the wrong app development partner it could result in the app having many issues such as the functionalities of the app is not working the way it should, the design of the app might be mediocre and not up to the expectations and standards set. This may also force the business to have to allocate additional funds towards finding a better app developer. The best way to avoid this risk is to avoid low-cost services and instead pay for a pricier app developer service that has a good reputation and guarantees customer satisfaction. 


     The third risk is security breaches, this is something that is very common to many mobile apps. Where hackers steal customers private information from businesses and then extort money from them by threatening to make their customers private information public. This can result in the business-facing multiple lawsuits filed against them by customers that had their private information leaked to the public, this would cost a large sum of money that would put them in a lot of debt and tarnish the businesses reputation that they built. 


     The Fourth risk is programming the robot to have all the features that it claims to have may be difficult despite hiring professional programmers. Bugs and errors in the system will still most definitely occur and perfecting the robot will be a long and continuous journey as more issues tend to arise when working on a project. It will require a lot of time and testing before it can be in service, to ensure that it is safe and adheres to all the legal requirements for having the delivery robot in use. 


     Another risk could be that the app will have little to no users because there are already many food delivery apps that are in service and have built a large userbase. It will be difficult to attract users of food delivery apps to use our service instead of a well-known and respectable food delivery services such as UberEATS, Menulog and DoorDash. It would be extremely difficult to enter the food delivery industry and build a reputation when there are already identical services that are available and popular. If there are no active customers then the business won’t be able to generate income, which will eventually lead the business to fail.
