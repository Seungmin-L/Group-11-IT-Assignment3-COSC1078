Group Reflection

 

 

Abdul’s Feedback

 

Q. What went well?

Team collaborations and group meeting discussions were good. Everyone was sure to keep everyone up to date with their work and was sure to ask for help where needed. This was much better than the last assignment as we were sure to improve on these specifically.

 

Q. What could be improved?

Preparations for team meetings could be improved. There were late meeting arrivals as well as unprepared update discussions so the punctuality can be improved much more.

 

Q. At least one thing that was surprising?

The quality of work as well as the time we were getting our work done was much better than assignment 2. We were a lot more prepared and collaborated a lot better than last time. 

 

Q. At least one thing that you have learned in groups?

Communication is always key; no work can be done effectively, and you can't gain help needed without communication. Keeping each other up to date is beneficial for everyone, that way we can plan our work out correctly and also gives room for improvements to our work producing work at a much higher quality.
